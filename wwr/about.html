Exciting Expeditions

Our goal is to provide you with the best experience possible. We have been providing unforgettable experiences for over 30 years. We are a family owned and operated business that is dedicated to giving you the safest, most memorable adventure possible. Our experienced guides are ready to help you make memories on the river!

History

Exciting Expeditions started floating the river in 1995. Since then, we’ve welcomed thousands of guests from across the world. Safety, stewardship, and pure fun have guided us from the first paddle stroke to today.

Adventure Awaits You

From gentle family floats to high-adrenaline runs, we have a trip for every skill level. Bring your spirit of adventure—we’ll take care of the rest.
